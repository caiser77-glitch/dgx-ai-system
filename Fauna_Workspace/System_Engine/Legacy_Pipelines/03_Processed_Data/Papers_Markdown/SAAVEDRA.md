RE-INTRODUCTION OF THE OTTER IN SPAIN 
 
Deli SAAVEDRA 
 
Fundacio Territori I Paisatge (Caixa Catalunya), Provenca 261-265 1 A, 08008 Barcelona, Spain. 
email: dsaavedra@lutra.jazztel.es 
 
 
 
The Eurasian otter disappeared from Muga and Fluvià basins due to hunting, pollution and habitat 
destruction. In 1993, a reintroduction project was initiated in order to restore the eradicated 
population and to promote rivers and wetlands conservation through a flag species. 41 Otters from 
W. Spain and Portugal were translocated to the releasing area, 93% of them fitted with 
radiotransmitters. 
 
Diverse data that prove the success of the reintroduction are presented, together with other 
aspects of spatial and temporal ecology of the established otter population.
